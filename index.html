About Us.

Hifi-Filter Private Limited was founded in 2021 with 25 years of past experience in oil filtration. We have our own captive manufacturing plant capable of making all types of filtration machines.
Our Values and policies have been customer centric and also cover the interest of all stake holders.
We have over 750 machines giving flawless service in Steel, Power, Auto and other process industries filtering more than 15 Lakh Liters of industrial oils every month on annual rate contract basis.
Taking an initiative under make in India we have developed Replacement / Substitute filters of all renowned makes.
We are preferred service provider in our field of operation by some of the large corporates like Tata Steel, SAIL, Jindal and tens of other major groups.
